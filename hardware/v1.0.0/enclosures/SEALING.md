# Sealing (dust + rain)

Target: **playa-practical** — IP67 on mated DT/DTP/M12, gasketed lids, HLG is factory IP67.  
Whole printed box is not claimed IP67 (C14 on inputs when unmated; arcade).

| Interface | Part | Mated | Unmated | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12 V | DTP 2-pin | IP67 at connector face | cavity | Pocket + foam; leave mated; cavity plugs |
| SOL | DT 2-pin | IP67 at connector face | same | Same; flange option if budget allows |
| RS-485 | M12-5 | IP67 (gasketed nut) | open | Dust caps |
| AC (inputs) | C14 | cord helps | ~IP20 | Cover when unmated |
| POWER (inputs) | KCD4 + boot | splash | boot | Silicone boot |
| 12 V PSU | HLG-240H-12 | **IP67 unit** | — | Mount shaded |
| LED window | PC + foam | splash/dust | — | Not an open slot |
| Lid | silicone gasket | splash/dust | — | Continuous gasket |
| Arcade | EG STARTS | not certified | nut/LED | Silicone under bezel |

## Rules

1. DT / DTP / M12 for outdoor signal and power links.
2. Kill 12 V by unplugging HLG AC (no AC-rated rocker in the 12 V path).
3. Printed pockets: compression lip + foam; do not claim panel IP67 without flange parts.
4. Cap unused M12; use DT/DTP cavity plugs.
5. LED = PC lens + foam. Lid = continuous silicone gasket.
